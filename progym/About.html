Our Journey

Our journey began with a passion for fitness and a desire to create a welcoming space where everyone could pursue their fitness goals. Founded in 2018 by Ahmad Rabah, PROGYM became a hub for individuals seeking not just physical strength but also mental resilience, and a supportive community.

Our Mission

"Empowering Lives Through Fitness" is not just our mission statement; it's the essence of everything we do. We believe that a healthy body and mind are the foundation for a fulfilling life. Our mission is to provide you with the tools, guidance, and support you need to achieve your fitness aspirations.

Our Values

Commitment to Excellence: We're dedicated to delivering top-notch fitness programs, state-of-the-art equipment, and professional coaching to help you reach your fitness goals.

Inclusivity

We welcome individuals of all fitness levels, backgrounds, and ages. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or taking your first step toward fitness, you'll find a home at PROGYM.

Community Spirit

Our gym is more than just a place to work out; it's a vibrant community where members motivate and inspire each other. Together, we celebrate victories and overcome challenges.

Health and Safety

Your well-being is our top priority. We maintain the highest standards of cleanliness, safety, and hygiene to ensure you can work out with confidence.

World-Class Facilities

Step inside PROGYM, and you'll discover a world of fitness possibilities. Our state-of-the-art facility boasts a wide range of cardio and strength equipment, group fitness studios, and much more. It's a space designed to elevate your fitness experience.
